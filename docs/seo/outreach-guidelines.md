# Zero-Email Community Seeding Guidelines

Traditional SEO relies heavily on "cold-emailing" blogs asking for links. In the automotive niche, this rarely works and often marks the brand as spam.

Instead, we use the **"Value-First Community Seeding"** model, leveraging the `Fitment Hub` as our primary link magnet.

## The Strategy

1. **Identify the Community:** 
   - Monitor high-traffic forums (*SilveradoSierra.com*, *GMFullSize.com*, *Reddit r/Silverado*, *F150forum.com*).
   - Look for active threads where users are asking questions like:
     - "What size is the grill bowtie on a 2018 LTZ?"
     - "Will a 2020 emblem fit my 2015?"
     - "How do I remove the rear badge without scratching the paint?"

2. **Provide Raw Value First (No Selling):**
   - Reply to the thread with the exact answer (e.g., "The front grille on the 2014-2018 standard trim is usually 11.25 x 4.0 inches with clips.")
   - Do NOT mention buying a product.
   - Do NOT post a link to a product page.

3. **Provide the 'Proof' Link:**
   - Follow your answer with a link to the overarching resource to prove your point.
   - *Example:* "I found a pretty good breakdown of all the sizes by generation here if you need to double-check: `[Link to Fitment Hub]`"

4. **The Network Effect:**
   - Because you solved the user's problem without trying to sell them something, forum moderators are highly unlikely to delete the link.
   - This link now lives forever on a high-authority domain (DR 40+), passing link juice to the Fitment Hub.
   - The Fitment Hub internally passes that link juice directly to the product pages via optimized anchor text (e.g., "chevrolet silverado emblems").

## Execution Checklist

- [ ] Create accounts on top 5 truck forums. Let them "age" for 1 week before posting links.
- [ ] Set up Google Alerts for "Chevy Bowtie Size", "How to remove Silverado Emblem", etc.
- [ ] Aim for 2 organic, value-add forum posts per week.

## Moderation Rules
- **Never PM (Private Message) users asking them to buy.**
- If someone explicitly asks "Where did you get that?", you are permitted to link the product page directly.
- **Tone:** Act as an enthusiast helping another enthusiast. State facts, provide measurements, and link the hub.
