## 5. Performance Guidelines
- **Rule of Threes:** Try to use at least 3 different anchor variations for your most important pages (Home, Shop, Top Category).
- **Natural Flow:** Ensure the sentence reads correctly. 
    - *Bad:* "We offer custom chevy bowties for sale."
    - *Good:* "If you’re looking for a one-of-a-kind look, our **Custom Chevy Bowties** are built to survive the toughest conditions."
- **Avoid Over-Optimization:** Do not link the same keywords on every single paragraph. One link per 300 words is a good benchmark.
